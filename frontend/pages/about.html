About Rana.bg

Rana.bg acts as the bridge between creative vision and technological precision. We engineered this platform to eliminate the complexity of image editing, making professional-grade background removal accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

To democratize design by providing a powerful, privacy-focused, and accessible AI tool. Whether you are a professional designer, a digital marketer, or a content creator, we believe that achieving a flawless image shouldn't require hours of manual labor.

The Technology

Powered by advanced machine learning models (U²-Net), Rana.bg detects subjects with pixel-perfect accuracy. We combine this automated intelligence with intuitive manual controls—giving you the speed of AI with the precision of a human editor.
